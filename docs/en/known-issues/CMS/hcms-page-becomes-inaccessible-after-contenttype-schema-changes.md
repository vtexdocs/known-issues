## Summary

After altering the schema of a content-type, the hCMS page may become inaccessible in the admin.

## Simulation

1. Alter the schema of a content-type in FastStore hCMS.
2. Edit any field in the **Settings** tab of the affected page and save.
3. The page becomes inaccessible in the admin.

## Workaround

If the page is already inaccessible, please contact VTEX Support.
