## Summary

Indexing History page in the Admin may show a high volume of products with a high indexing delay, affecting the update of the product information in the storefront.

## Simulation

1. On **VTEX Admin**, go to the **Indexing History** page.
2. In the **Indexing status share** block, there is a high number of products stuck in the `Updating` status for many hours.

## Workaround

Open a ticket to request a full reindex.
