## Summary


Orders remain in Payment Pending even though the transaction has already been approved. The transaction advances to the approved status and sends the corresponding notification to OMS; however, when OMS checks the transaction status, it is shown as authorized instead of approved.


#### Simulation


It is not possible to simulate.

Open a ticket for the product support team
