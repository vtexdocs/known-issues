## Summary

Subscription promotions with a specific periodicity configured may be applied correctly to the initial order, but fail to apply to the subsequent recurring orders.

## Simulation

1. Create a promotion that applies to subscription orders.
2. Configure the promotion with a specific periodicity (for example, daily, weekly or monthly)
3. Create a subscription purchase that matches the promotion criteria.
4. Verify that the discount is applied in the first order.
5. Wait for the next recurring order generated by the subscription flow.
6. Check that the promotion is no longer applied, even though the subscription should still match the configured criteria.
