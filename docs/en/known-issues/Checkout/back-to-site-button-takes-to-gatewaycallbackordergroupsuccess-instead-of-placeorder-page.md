## Summary


When the customer makes a purchase using a giftcard and a payment app, the “return to the site” button or flow to go back to the site does not take them to the place order page, but to `/gatewayCallback//Success`


#### Simulation



1. Put the giftcard of any value
2. Select the method "payment app"
3. Inside the payment app, make a purchase
4. Go back to VTEX environment.


#### Workaround


_There is no workaround_
